Shrimp Aglio e Olio

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 200g Shrimp
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Sauté shrimp separately (2–3 min).
  2. Prepare garlic oil.
  3. Add pasta + shrimp.
  4. Toss well.
