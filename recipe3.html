Penne Arrabbiata

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Penne
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, sauté garlic.
  2. Add tomato puree → cook 5–7 min.
  3. Add chili flakes + salt.
  4. Toss boiled pasta.
  5. Cook till sauce coats pasta.
