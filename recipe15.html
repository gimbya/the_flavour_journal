Churros with Chocolate Sauce

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate

Instructions

  1. Cook water + butter → add flour dough.
  2. Pipe into hot oil, fry.
  3. Coat with sugar.
  4. Serve with melted chocolate.
