Aglio e Olio

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti; preserve pasta water.
  2. Heat olive oil (low heat).
  3. Add garlic → cook till lightly golden.
  4. Add chili flakes.
  5. Toss pasta + little pasta water.
  6. Garnish with parsley.
