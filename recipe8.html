Chicken Arrabbiata

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Penne
  • 200g chicken
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken separately.
  2. Prepare spicy tomato sauce.
  3. Add pasta + chicken.
  4. Mix and simmer.
