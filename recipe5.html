Vegetable Lasagna

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup white sauce
  • 1 cup cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Layer: sheets → tomato sauce → veggies → white sauce → cheese.
  3. Repeat layers.
  4. Bake 25–30 min till golden.
