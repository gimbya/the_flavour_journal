Panna Cotta

Preparation Time: 15 min (+ chilling)

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 cup cream
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp gelatin
  • Vanilla essence

Instructions

  1. Heat cream + milk + sugar.
  2. Add dissolved gelatin.
  3. Add vanilla.
  4. Pour into moulds.
  5. Chill till set.
