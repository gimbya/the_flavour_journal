Fettuccine Alfredo

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3–4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup cream
  • ½ cup parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water; preserve ½ cup pasta water.
  2. Melt butter, sauté garlic (low heat).
  3. Add cream, simmer gently.
  4. Add cheese, stir till smooth.
  5. Add pasta + pasta water → mix well.
  6. Season and serve.
