Pesto Pasta

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp nuts (pine nuts/cashew)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup cheese
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Grind basil, nuts, garlic, oil → smooth paste.
  2. Boil pasta.
  3. Mix pesto with hot pasta.
  4. Add cheese and toss.
