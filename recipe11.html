Tiramisu

Preparation Time: 20 min (+ chilling)

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Ladyfinger biscuits
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • ½ cup cream
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Dip biscuits quickly in coffee.
  2. Whip cream + sugar + mascarpone.
  3. Layer: biscuits → cream → repeat.
  4. Dust cocoa powder on top.
  5. Chill for 4–6 hours.
