Chocolate Mousse

Preparation Time: 15 min (+ chilling)

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate.
  2. Fold in whipped cream gently.
  3. Add sugar if needed.
  4. Chill for 2–3 hours.
