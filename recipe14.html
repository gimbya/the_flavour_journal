No-Bake Cheesecake

Preparation Time: 25 min (+ chilling)

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Biscuit crumbs
  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Cream

Instructions

  1. Mix biscuit + butter → base.
  2. Beat cream cheese + sugar + cream.
  3. Pour over base.
  4. Chill 4-5 hours.
